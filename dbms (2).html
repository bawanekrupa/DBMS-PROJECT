The "Brain" Behind the Beauty

What is a DBMS?

It's a software layer (like MySQL or PostgreSQL) that manages your college's memories. It ensures Data Integrity—meaning Maya can't be in two places at once!

Why Relational?

Because everything is connected. A student's ID connects to their Grades, which connects to their Professor. It's a web of information!
